Traveling

Introduction

At some point, just about everyone figures out that traveling isn’t difficult. You book a flight, hotel, and look up some things to do. Maybe you buy a package and everything is planned for you. You go, see the top sights, then leave. It’s instagramable and you see some pretty cool things. But, this never seemed enough to me. For some reason, my parents didn’t like the feeling of being a tourist. So, growing up, I never really got the chance. The first time we left the country was for 5 months. The second time I left was 2 years. The next time was 5 months again. The point was really seeing the country, experiencing the culture, speaking some of the language, and eating the food. This blog is to talk about the experiences trying to experience the culture during traveling, instead of just the beaches or restaurants or “top 10 destinations”.
